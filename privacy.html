Privacy Policy

Dog Vibes Dog Training · Effective Date: March 22, 2026 · Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Dog Vibes Dog Training ("Dog Vibes," "we," "us," or "our") is a sole proprietorship owned and operated by Kevin Clarke, based in Portland, Oregon. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over your data. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

a. Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact information — your name, email address, and phone number when you fill out our intake form or contact us
  • Dog information — your dog's name, breed, age, health history, and behavioral details submitted through our intake survey
  • Payment information — billing details processed through Stripe (we do not store your full card number; Stripe handles all payment data)
  • Communications — any messages you send us via email or contact forms

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Website analytics — basic usage data such as pages visited, browser type, and approximate location. This data is aggregated and not linked to your identity.

c. Information from Scheduling

  • Scheduling data — when you book a consultation through Calendly, we receive your name, email, and appointment details, which sync to Google Calendar.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Schedule and conduct dog training consultations (in-person or via Google Meet)
  • Personalize training recommendations based on your dog's profile
  • Send confirmation and follow-up emails related to your appointments
  • Process payments for services
  • Send newsletters or training tips via Mailchimp (only if you opt in)
  • Improve our services and respond to your inquiries

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising or share it with third-party marketers.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

5. Email Marketing

If you opt in to receive emails from Dog Vibes (such as training tips or newsletters), we use Mailchimp to manage and send those emails. Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time.

We comply with the CAN-SPAM Act. Our emails will always include:

  • Our physical mailing address (Portland, OR)
  • A clear way to unsubscribe
  • Honest subject lines

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as we have an active client relationship with you, or as required for legitimate business purposes (such as tax records). If you request deletion of your data, we will remove it from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

Oregon Residents: Under the Oregon Consumer Privacy Act (OCPA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we hold about you
  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any sale of personal data (we do not sell data)

California Residents: Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have similar rights including the right to know, delete, and opt out of sale.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 45 days.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • All tools we use (Google, Stripe, Notion, Calendly, etc.) use industry-standard encryption (TLS/HTTPS)
  • We use strong, unique passwords and two-factor authentication on all accounts
  • Access to client records in Notion is restricted to Kevin Clarke only
  • We do not store payment card numbers — Stripe handles all payment security

No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you promptly.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
